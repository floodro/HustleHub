HustleHub - Enhancing Part-Time Job Opportunities for Students and Individuals through a Mobile Application

Description:
- HustleHub is a mobile job-search application that helps individuals look for part-time jobs and opportunities based on their 
skills, and experiences.

Possible Features:
- Job Posting Hub (Users can look for specific jobs based on their preferences, experience, and skills)

- Professional Profile (Job-seekers can have their portfolios and their credentials listed in their user profile, so 
companies can have an easier time to identify whether or not the person seeking a job is qualified to handle
a specific work.)

- Worker Ratings (Companies can identify a worker's rating and leave a review depending on that person's performance,
attitude, etc.)
